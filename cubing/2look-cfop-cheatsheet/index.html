Move Triggers — the finger-trick vocabulary

Right-hand dominant, cube in a neutral grip. These build almost every alg above — drill them until they're one motion, not four. Base flicks: U = left index push · U' = right index push · R = right hand flicks the R-face up · R' = right hand flicks it down · F = left thumb pushes the front up · F' = left fingers push the front down.

TriggerMovesWhat it doesFinger sequence
Sexy move R U R' U' The universal building block — one 4-move burst that cycles three pieces. Shows up in the OLL cross algs, the T cases, and most PLLs here. R right ring flicks up → U left index → R' right index flicks down → U' right index. Learn this one first.
Reverse sexy U R U' R' Sexy run backwards; it exactly undoes a sexy move. Useful to recognize so you can read algs in either direction. U left index → R right ring up → U' right index → R' right index down.
Sune trigger R U R' U A sexy move whose final turn is U instead of U'. Add R U2 R' and you've got Sune; the H-OLL is three of these bursts in a row. Identical to sexy until the last move — finish with a left-index U push instead of the right-index U'.
Sledgehammer R' F R F' The other workhorse — inserts/cycles corners. Alternates a right-hand move with a left-hand F move. Ends the Diagonal PLL and (wide) the T-OLL. R' right index flicks down → F left thumb pushes front up → R right ring up → F' left fingers push front down.
Hedgeslammer F R' F' R Sledgehammer reversed (a.k.a. "hedge"). Same two hands, opposite order — undoes a sledgehammer. Opens the L-OLL (wide-ended). F left thumb up → R' right index down → F' left fingers down → R right ring up.
Wide sexy r U R' U' A sexy whose first turn is a wide R (drags the middle slice along). Same rhythm as sexy — this is the opener of the T-OLL. Grip the top two layers with the right hand for the first flick, then finish exactly like a sexy move.
M-slice flick M' · M2 Powers the M-slice edge perms (H, Z, and the alternate Ua/Ub). M follows the L direction; M2 is a double flick. Left hand grips F/B; right ring finger flicks the bottom-back of the middle slice for M' — for M2, roll ring then middle in one motion. Alternate with left-index U pushes.
Notation: R L U D F B = clockwise face turn · ' = counter-clockwise · 2 = 180° · r f = wide turn (face + adjacent slice) · M = middle slice (same direction as L) · S = slice between F and B (same direction as F) · x y z = whole-cube rotation  |  Yellow squares = sticker facing top.
