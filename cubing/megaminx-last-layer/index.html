Megaminx Last Layer — J Perm Beginner Method

The last-layer steps from J Perm's How to Solve a Megaminx! [Beginner Tutorial]. Hold the puzzle with the gray face on top (opposite your starting white face). Every step is one short algorithm you re-angle (AUF) and repeat until that step is done — first the five edges (the star), then the five corners. Diagrams show the gray face from above.

Additional & Alternate Algorithms

Extra cases and directions for the corner steps (standard, well-documented megaminx algorithms). The beginner method works with just the four algs on page 1 — these save re-doing an alg several times when you can read the case directly.

Notation: R L U F B = clockwise turn of that face · ' = counter-clockwise · 2 = 180°  (U2' is a 180° turn done anticlockwise — same result as U2).  |  Diagram key: / gray = piece oriented (gray on top) · dark = not yet oriented · light = don't-care this step · round = edge, pentagon = corner · arrows = the 3-cycle the algorithm performs.
